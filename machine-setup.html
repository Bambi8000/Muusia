PRESETS

A preset just fills sensible starting values — tune everything below.

IDENTITY & WORK AREA

PEN LIFT (Z) — the important part for bed plotters

On the Ultimaker S5 the whole heated bed is your Z axis — heavy and slow. Two settings tame this:
• Z-hop lifts only a hair (Contact + Z-hop) between nearby paths instead of the full Lift height. Full lift is used only at pen changes.
• Settle delays pause after the bed moves so the pen doesn't drag a line before the bed has stopped. A servo needs almost none of this; a bed needs 80–150 ms. Start high, reduce until you see artifacts.
• Lower Z feed if lifts overshoot or knock; raise it once motion is smooth.

SPEEDS

First plot advice: set Draw F low (600–1200) so a skipped line is a slow, cancellable mistake rather than a fast scribble. Speed up once the pen behaves.

PEN CHANGE & G-CODE FRAMING

Start runs once at the top (units, absolute mode, homing). End runs last (park). M0 pauses for a manual pen swap; some firmwares prefer M600 or a custom macro.

BRUSH ROTATION (optional)

Emits Klipper MANUAL_STEPPER moves so a flat brush stays tangent to the stroke. Leave off for a normal pen.

DIP / RE-INK (optional)

For dip pens / ink wells: every N mm of drawing the pen parks over the well, plunges, waits, and resumes. Ignore for markers and ballpoints.
